Privacy Policy — MemoCalc

Effective date: June 19, 2026 · Version 1.1 · Publisher: ThangLong Intl

MemoCalc (the "app") is a calculation notebook for individuals and small businesses. MemoCalc is built on the principle that your data stays on your device. This policy explains what data is processed, where, and your rights.

1. Data stored on your device only

Everything you create in MemoCalc is stored locally on your device. MemoCalc has no backend server and never uploads the following:

DataPurpose
Calculation notes in progress (multi-tab)Auto-saved so nothing is lost when you close the app
Saved work historyReview and reopen past work
Quick List (items, prices, groups)Insert reusable values quickly into notes
Business profile (company name, tax ID, phone, address, bank details, logo)Printed on PDF quotes you generate
Quote draftKeeps your draft when you leave the Quote Editor
Saved PDF filesPDF Library
Display settings (font size, line height, language, currency)Interface preferences

Documents scanned with the Scan feature are processed entirely on-device to produce a PDF file. Neither images nor document content are transmitted over the network.

2. Data processed by third-party services

2.1 Firebase Authentication

Sign-in is optional. If you choose to sign in with Google, Firebase processes your email address, display name, and profile photo solely for authentication. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

2.2 RevenueCat

Manages paid plan entitlements. Processes an anonymous user ID, purchase history, and entitlement status (Pro or Remove Ads). Payment is handled entirely by Google Play — RevenueCat never receives your payment details. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

2.3 Google AdMob

Shown to free-tier users only. May process the device Advertising ID and device information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Users who have purchased Pro or Remove Ads do not see ads and are not subject to ad-related data processing.

3. Permissions

PermissionReason
CameraScan documents to PDF using the Scan feature
Photo librarySelect a business logo image for your profile
InternetOptional Google sign-in, ads (free tier), subscription verification
In-app BillingPro and Remove Ads purchases via Google Play

MemoCalc does not access contacts, location, microphone, or messages.

4. Paid plans

MemoCalc offers two upgrade options, both processed through Google Play:

  • Pro — subscription (monthly / yearly / lifetime): unlocks all Pro features and removes ads.
  • Remove Ads — one-time purchase: removes ads only, does not unlock Pro features.

Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled before the next billing period via Google Play. RevenueCat stores your entitlement status so purchases can be restored on a new device.

5. Account and data deletion

  • In-app: Settings → Account → Delete account — permanently deletes the linked Firebase account (email, name, profile photo) from our systems.
  • On-device data: uninstall the app or clear app data in Android Settings.
  • By email: send a request to contact@thanglongintl.com — processed within 30 days.
  • Your Pro or Remove Ads purchase is tied to your Google Play account and can be restored after Firebase account deletion.

6. Children

MemoCalc is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Security and retention

Local data is stored in the app's Android-protected private sandbox. Data held by Firebase and RevenueCat is retained under their respective security standards and removed when you delete your account.

8. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
